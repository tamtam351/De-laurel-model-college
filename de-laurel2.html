About us

Welcome to De-Laurel Model College

At De-Laurel Model College, we are dedicated to nurturing the minds of the next generation in a vibrant and inclusive environment. Founded on the principles of excellence, integrity, and compassion, our school serves as a beacon of knowledge and character for students and the community alike. Our mission is to provide a holistic education that fosters academic achievement, personal growth, and social responsibility. We believe that every student possesses unique talents and abilities, and we strive to cultivate these through a rigorous curriculum and diverse extracurricular activities.

Our Vision

De-Laurel Model College envisions a future where our students become leaders, innovators, and responsible citizens. We aim to empower them with critical thinking skills, creativity, and a strong ethical foundation, preparing them to thrive in an ever-changing world.
